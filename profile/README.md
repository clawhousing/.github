# ClawHousing

**Your Personal AI Assistant, Hosted.**

[ClawHousing](https://clawhousing.com) is a managed hosting platform for [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) — the open-source personal AI assistant. We handle the infrastructure so you can focus on what your assistant does, not how it runs.

## What We Do

- **Instant setup** — Sign up, pick a plan, and your AI assistant is live in seconds. No servers, no Docker, no terminal.
- **8 messaging channels** — WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, Signal, iMessage, Google Chat, Microsoft Teams. Connect any or all of them.
- **7 AI models** — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Llama, DeepSeek, Qwen, Kimi. Switch anytime, all included.
- **Browser automation** — Your assistant can browse the web, fill forms, extract data, and automate workflows.
- **50+ MCP integrations** — GitHub, Google Drive, Notion, databases, APIs, and more. All managed through a dashboard.
- **Scheduled tasks** — Set up cron jobs to trigger your assistant on a schedule.
- **Persistent memory** — Your assistant remembers context across conversations and channels.
